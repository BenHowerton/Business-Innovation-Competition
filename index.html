Schedule

Tracks & Application Requirements

  • Participant Team Application Deadline: Sunday, Oct 26, 2025, 11:59 PM (MDT)
  • Application Package contents required:
    • Early Stage Track: 60‑second video
    • Open Track: Full Business Model Canvas (BMC) with financials
  • Two competition tracks: Early Stage (idea pitches) and Open Track (more developed ventures)

Early Stage Idea Pitch

  • 60‑second video submission
  • Some progress with customer discovery
  • Clear target customer articulation
  • Early product/market fit explanation

Open Track Developed Venture

  • Full BMC (Business Model Canvas) explanation
  • Related financials (required)
  • Assumes deeper validation and traction
  • Suitable for teams beyond ideation

Event Details

  • Location: Campus-wide, Boise State University, Boise, ID 83725
  • Eligibility: Open to ALL Boise State students. Undergraduate and graduate students are encouraged.
  • Prizes: $10,000 in prize money available
  • Challenge: Deliver your best business venture pitch!

Benefits of Participation

  • Gain recognition for your innovative ideas.
  • Network with industry professionals and like‑minded peers.
  • Connect with potential partners and mentors.
  • Compete for prize money and potential funding opportunities.
